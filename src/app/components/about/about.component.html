About Liubov M

Liubov is a pastry chef with experience creating different types of culinary products. From simple traditional cookies to elaborately decorated cakes – Liubov is certain to come up with a masterpiece. Her inspiration takes origin from family cookbooks, cooking classes and – most importantly – her creative mind. She considers herself a “forever student”, never missing a chance to learn a new skill and never ceasing to amaze.

Most recently, her desire for knowledge has brought her to Paris, where she met the chefs from “The Ritz” for a master class of Madeleines – famous French treats. Next year, Liubov is planning to pursue her studies in Canada and further expand the knowledge of the culinary world.
